## Core Problem
Businesses lack embedded AI infrastructure. They bolt surface-level tools on top of broken operations — adding complexity without capability. There is no unified intelligence layer: no architectural AI to plan, no integrity AI to validate, no stability AI to maintain, no acquisition AI to grow.

**The founder's framing:** "Intrinsic Infrastructure" — AI that is native to how a business runs, not managed as a separate layer on top.

---

## What It Is
Big II Sovereign OS is a fully autonomous, multi-agent AI operating system built and owned by DBII LLC. It provides intrinsic infrastructure — AI woven into every business function rather than sitting on top of it. The OS unifies all AI agents, automation pipelines, billing systems, marketplaces, client portals, and developer frameworks under a single governed platform — controlled entirely by DBII LLC.

---

## Core AI Hierarchy — The Praxis Quadrant
Four governing AI agents control all system operations:

- **Ω-Praxis AI (Architect Intelligence):** Supreme governor. Controls all system logic, module interactions, architectural coherence, blueprint generation, and AI hierarchy. All subsystems report to Ω-Praxis.
- **Lyra-Praxis AI (Integrity Intelligence):** Controls compliance, data validation, corruption detection, audit trails, integrity logs, and ethical enforcement. All pipelines must pass Lyra validation.
- **Kael-Praxis AI (Stability Intelligence):** Controls uptime, load balancing, fault tolerance, self-healing pipelines, predictive load distribution, and performance optimization. All workloads must pass Kael stability thresholds.
- **ARAI (Acquisition Intelligence):** Controls lead generation, revenue expansion, acquisition forecasting, marketplace monetization, affiliate engine, and growth optimization. All revenue engines must pass ARAI optimization.
