**Tagline:** The Consulting AI Operating System
**Category:** Multi-Agent AI Operating System / Enterprise SaaS Platform

## Core Problem
Businesses lack embedded AI infrastructure. They bolt surface-level tools on top of broken operations — adding complexity without capability. No unified intelligence layer: no architectural AI to plan, no integrity AI to validate, no stability AI to maintain, no acquisition AI to grow.

## Day-One Feature (MVP Beachhead)
White-label onboarding wizard: a reseller logs in, sets their brand (logo, colors, domain, AI names) and connects their PayPal account, and has a fully live branded portal ready to sell to clients — within minutes. No dev work. No technical setup. Instant reseller activation.

## Primary Users
- Founders — need AI infrastructure embedded into how they run, sell, and scale
- Agency Owners — need autonomous consulting delivery, lead gen, and client management
- Enterprise Ops Teams — need governed, compliant, stable AI pipelines at scale
- White-Label Resellers — need a branded OS to resell, keeping the markup

## The Praxis Quadrant — Four AI Governors
- Ω-Praxis: Architect Intelligence — governs all system logic, blueprints, AI hierarchy
- Lyra: Integrity Intelligence — compliance, validation, audit trails, integrity logs
- Kael: Stability Intelligence — uptime, load balancing, self-healing pipelines
- ARAI: Acquisition Intelligence — lead gen, revenue expansion, acquisition forecasting
